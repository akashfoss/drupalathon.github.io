ABOUT

This is an initiative to make students aware of Drupal, and to explore and be in touch with the community by contributing to the Drupal Core. The students would get exposed to basic functionalities like Issue Queues, the Drupal Ladder and some of the Core projects to work on to learn more about Drupal and to start their contribution towards this exciting world of Content Management. The attenedees will be exposed to the various cutting edge technologies of Drupal, its architecture, site building, writing custom modules and also the vast lying job opportunities connected with this enormous organisation.
